Towers of Glass

May 23, 2020

Tokyo has many stunning towers of glass, and this one, the newish Kitte building on the south side of Marunouchi Plaza, is one of my favourites. Here looking especially beautiful against a clear blue winter sky.

The viewing platform from where this photograph was taken is the perfect location for looking out over the plaza and Tokyo Station too.
