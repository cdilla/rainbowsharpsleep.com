Tokyo through my eyes and lens

Sunny Minato

Feb 14, 2021

It is interesting to see how many of the buildings around Tokyo Tower are not as tall as you might expect. Here, looking closer along Sakurada-dori south through Minato City towards Takanawa, the apartment and business buildings seem to rise as they get further away. It may not be deliberate, but it does have the happy consequence of making for many wonderful views from the Tower.

It was a lovely sunny day when I took this, making me want to join the pedestrians down on the sunlit pavement.
